## 📝 Gmail Rules

- ✅ Letters (a-z)
- ✅ Numbers (0-9)
- ✅ Single periods (.) but avoid multiple periods in a row
- ✅ Must be 6-30 characters total
- ❌ No special characters like hyphens (-), underscores (_), etc.
- ❌ No consecutive periods

## 💡 Quick Steps
